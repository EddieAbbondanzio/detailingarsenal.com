Features

With every feature carefully designed, Detailing Arsenal has everything you need and nothing you don't. Each time a feature is added we assess it's use cases to ensure it'll help detailers run their business as best as possible.

Appointment Templates

Appointments can be created from service templates that can keep track of prices, and average time to complete. We also offer the ability to add multiple prices for each service so a single service can have a different prices for vehicles based on size.

Multi-day Appointments

Sometimes larger jobs such as paint corrections, or extremely in-depth full details may take more than a day to complete. That's why we support the ability to create appointments across multiple days.

Mobile Optimizied

Mobile devices are fully supported. We've designed our services to be fully functional for any phone, tablet, or laptop. We want to help mobile detailers on the go, or detailers working inside a shop.
